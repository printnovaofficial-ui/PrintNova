FAQ

File types, materials, and turnaround.

Which file types do you accept?

STL and 3MF are accepted. Geometry-based auto-weight currently works for STL uploads on the public site.

What happens if I do not upload a file?

You can still place the order. The site shows a disclaimer and support follows up within 24 hours.

Which materials are available?

PLA, PETG, ABS, and TPU are listed in the estimator. Available colors can be updated from the admin page.

What are the size limits?

Single-part jobs should stay within 220mm × 220mm × 250mm. Bigger models need splitting.

Terms and refund policy
